GANG Culture Online
What is GANG ?

"GANG" is a word in Chinese
which could be understood in the meaning of
"argue", "dispute", "bicker" or "wrangle".

However, unlike normal argument,
it is a derogatory term in Chinese describing that
someone argues for the sake of arguing.

People who like to GANG to others all the time
are called GANG JING in Chinese.

They are always high-profile
and good at destroying others' good mood
and good atmospheres.

Worse, when you want to blame them
for their remarks, they will say that
they are just saying.

How they GANG to others ?

Usually, GANG on the Internet happens, for example, when you add comments below the video, leave messages in the forum, or share your life in social media, and suddenly someone jumps out and GANG to you. He/She will contradicts or attacks you with the theory that seems reasonable at first glance but actually totally in the wrong logic.

Some people will refute you
whatever you say, which is GANG.

Most of the time, they argue with you
not for the correct point of view,
but to attact the attention of others.

This is why their comments are usually illogical and distorted,
because their aim is not reaching the truth,
but to gain the sense of superiority
by suppressing others' opinions.

How to respond to GANG JING ?

Firstly, never argue with anyone who GANG to you.

Arguing with him/her will let them more excited,
so you need to calm down and debate with him/her logically.

Then, if he/she cannot listen to you,
you would be better just ignore him/her.

The most imprtant part is that keep calm all the time.
